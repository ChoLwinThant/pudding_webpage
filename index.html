Caramel Pudding


Easy egg pudding

(with simple ingredients)


This is a dessert which is very easy to make with simple ingredients.

Contains : Egg, Milk, Water, Sugar

Allergens : Egg, Milk


Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sweetened condense Milk (optional)

Tools

  • mixing bowl
  • frypan or saucepan
  • pot with lid
  • mould (which can fit inside your pot)
  • aluminium foil or plate (which can fit inside your pot) to cover the mould

Step-by-step instructions

1) Making caramel sauce

In a frypan or saucepan, mix 1/4 cup of sugar and 2 tablespoons of water. Boil it with medium heat until the sugar melted and turns brown. Then, pour the sauce into the mould.


2) Boiling the milk

Boil 2 cups of milk with 1/2 cup of sugar for 5-7 minutes. You can also add more sugar or condense milk if you like sweet while boiling. Then cool it down until it is lukewarm.


3) Mix with eggs

Beat 5 eggs in a bowl and blend with the lukewarm milk.

*The milk should not be hot when it is blended with eggs.


4) Steaming

Pour the mixure into the mould that was prepared with caramel in step 1. Cover the mould with some aluminium foil or a plate. Put the mould in the pot and steam with hot water for 20-25 minutes.


5) Cool it down

Take out the mould and cool it down at room temperature first. You can serve it warm or you can refrigerate it for a few hours.

*To unmold, run a knife around rim of the mould and invert onto dessert plate.
